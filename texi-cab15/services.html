Non-Emergency Medical Transport Services
Available Throughout San Diego & East County

El Cajon • Lakeside • Santee • La Mesa • Spring Valley • San Carlos • San Diego • Jamul • Alpine

All Access Medical Transport is available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, to provide reliable and affordable non-emergency medical transportation services throughout San Diego and East County. With our experienced drivers and clean, modern shuttle vans with wheelchair lifts, we're able to make it easier, safer, and more efficient for senior citizens and people with disabilities to get wherever they need to go.

All Access Medical Transport Provides Wheelchair-Accessible Transportation To & From:

  • Medical offices
  • Dental offices
  • Cancer treatment centers
  • Dialysis centers
  • Eye institutes
  • Physical therapy clinics
  • Pharmacies
  • Medical supply stores
  • Nursing facilities
  • Government offices
  • Family gatherings
  • Social events
  • Salons
  • Grocery stores
  • Shopping malls
  • Churches
  • Airports
  • Bus/train stations

You Can Always Count on Exceptional Service When You Ride With All Access Medical Transport

When you call All Access, you will always get an experienced driver who is thoroughly trained and certified in CPR and first aid. Every van driver we employ is licensed to drive in California and is required to have a clean driving record. Additionally, our drivers must pass all required background checks, drug and alcohol tests, and medical examinations.

We like to get to know our customers and will provide as much assistance as they want or need. From delivering door to door service, helping passengers enter and exit the van, to ensuring that wheelchairs are always carefully loaded and fully secured before departing, we always go the extra mile to ensure that every ride with us is safe and comfortable.
