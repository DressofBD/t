About Us

All Access Medical Transport is a licensed, professional non-emergency medical transportation (NEMT) provider that serves San Diego, El Cajon, Lakeside, Santee, La Mesa, Spring Valley, San Carlos, and surrounding areas of East County, California. We're locally owned and operated and driven to provide the highest quality non-emergency medical transportation services in San Diego County.

Reasons to Choose All Access Medical Transport

Qualified Drivers - Our drivers are known for their professionalism, punctuality, and friendliness. Every driver on staff is licensed, insured, and CPR certified. Additionally, we require our drivers to have prior experience, pass a thorough background check and drug screening, and maintain a clean driving record.

Clean & Modern Wheelchair-Accessible Vans - Our vans are equipped with the latest safety features and ADA-compliant wheelchair lifts from BraunAbility. We keep our vehicles clean, well maintained, and 100% smoke-free, and sanitize them after every drop-off.

24/7 Availability - We know that some medical treatments are scheduled around the clock, which is why we offer our medical transport services 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Exceptional Service - We make it our top priority to treat every passenger with the utmost care and respect. You can count on our drivers to confirm all pick-up/drop-off locations and routes to ensure that you arrive on time for your appointment. Our driver will also gladly greet you at the door and provide additional assistance to help you safely board the vehicle and walk up or down stairs.

Great Value - Although we offer exceptional service, we have kept our rates very competitive. Call today to get a free quote!
