San Diego & East County's Experts in Non-Emergency Medical Transportation

All Access Medical Transportation is your best choice for non-emergency medical transportation in San Diego and East County. Our locally owned, fully licensed, and insured business is dedicated to providing high quality, affordable wheelchair-accessible transportation services throughout El Cajon, Lakeside, Santee, La Mesa, Spring Valley, San Carlos, and San Diego. Our services are designed to make it safe, convenient, and economical for people with mobility challenges and other disabilities to get to and from healthcare-related appointments, participate in social functions, and run errands.

Whether you need transportation services for dialysis treatment three times a week, a one-way ride to the train station, or a mid-day hair salon appointment, you can rely on All Access Medical Transportation.
